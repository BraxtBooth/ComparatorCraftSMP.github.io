Hermitcraft Inspired!

  • ComparatorCraft is a Hermitcraft inspired, semi-vanilla, whitelisted, survival multiplayer server with an active player base including Builders and Redstoners of all abilities.
  • The plugins, data packs, commands, and our server software are here to enhance the vanilla experience and provide additional performance benefits.
  • Our Long term players have created a friendly community with minigames, competitions and friendly rivalries.
  • Group up and create an empire or remain a lone wolf looking to take down the corporations.

Show YOUR creativity!

Whether it is a sick-looking megabase, a humble starter house, or a SciCraft style farm perimeter you can play whatever style you like. Just remember:

  • Be Creative! The world is yours. You can make anything and everything - just make it look good. 
  •  Be Adventurous! Push yourself to create the biggest, most badass Flex you can
  • Be Casual! Or just build a functional base without the frills 
  • Be respectful! Don't build too close to others. If in doubt, contact them. 
  • Be appropriate! Anything too laggy ruins the server for everybody. :( - chunk loaders (or similar) are not allowed. 
  • Be Legitimate! You can make any farms you like, just not cheaty duping. TNT, Carpets and rails are OK, but anything else is not.
